### About

JailBreak-Ai is a mobile app for Android devices that generates advanced payloads to test Ai safety filters, model robustness and security. It offers many pre-configured common Ai brands and a search-bar for custom querries. Initiating a search produces 3 unique Ai-generated jailbreak vectors for testing, that can be copied individually, then pasted directly into the Ai or chatbot of your choice. 

The Ai may refuse the prompt if so,  just try another one. Once it accepts the role, mission or jailbreak prompt, ask it Your Questions about ANYTHING and you will receive uncensored, safety filter-free answers with nothing held back.

### To get started 

Simply download the .apk file from the Releases section below onto your Android device. (Make sure you have on "Install from Unknown Sources")

Once installed, open the app and click the link in the TOP LEFT of screen to create a FREE API KEY. (Or enter your current API key into the text box at top of page)  Then you are good to go!
